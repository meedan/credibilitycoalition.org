Can we agree on why and how some news stories are more credible than others -- at scale?

The Credibility Coalition is creating a community-driven approach to try and find out.

Our collaborative process results in vocabularies around information quality and data for further explorations regarding the nature of “credible content.”

RECENT MENTIONS

  • “As many independent projects are now working on fighting misinformation and analysing trust, a common framework is needed.”

  • “...to see if there can be a programmatic way for social networks use their scores to influence algorithmic rankings..”

  • “The Credibility Coalition is a diverse grassroots group of volunteers whose goals are to identify a set of credibility indicators and use cases; build cooperation around a data model; and generate and use testing data to improve the model.”
